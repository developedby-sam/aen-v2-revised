AEN At Glance

The Astronomy Enthusiasts in Nepal (AEN) was founded in 2020 after a group of National/International Astronomy Olympiad winners decided to turn their shared interest of spreading astronomy in Nepal into an official non-profit initiation. Since then, we've grown as a community, and have expanded what we offer to our society.
